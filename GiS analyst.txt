GIS means Geographic Information Systems

A GIS (Geographic Information Systems) Analyst is a professional who uses GIS technology and spatial analysis to help organizations make informed decisions. 
GIS analysts work with geographic data, such as maps and satellite imagery, to create visual representations of information and analyze patterns 
and relationships in the data.



GIS analysts play an important role in a variety of industries by using geographic data and spatial analysis to help organizations make informed decisions.
